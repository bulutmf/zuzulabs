# Privacy Policy for Letter Leap: Word Hunt

**Last Updated:** March 2026

Zuzu Lab ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and the privacy of your children. We created *Letter Leap: Word Hunt* to be a safe, educational environment. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection practices.

## 1. Privacy 
Our app is designed for any age. 
* **We do not collect, store, or transmit any Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**.
* **We do not track location data.**
* **We do not use third-party analytics or advertising networks** that collect user data.

## 2. Local Data Storage
All game progress, unlocked levels, and scores are stored entirely locally on your device using Apple's native data frameworks (SwiftData). We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## 3. In-App Purchases and Links
*Letter Leap: Word Hunt* does not contain hidden in-app purchases. There is no external links within the app.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us.
